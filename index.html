Who Am I

Background, skills, and what drives me

I am Mostafa Aly, a high school student, IT administrator at Arabian Milling Company, and the founder of Tasklyst.io. I am an aspiring technologist with a strong focus on web development and cybersecurity, actively building both practical experience and technical depth at an early stage in my career.

Technical foundation

My technical foundation includes front-end development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, alongside programming experience in Python and C++. I also work with Linux systems and Bash scripting, and I am continuously expanding my knowledge in system-level computing, security concepts, and modern development tools. In addition, I have exposure to creative and productivity tools such as Photoshop and AI-based technologies, which I integrate into my workflow when needed.

How I learn

I learn through a highly practical approach—combining structured video learning with self-driven experimentation and real-world application. This hands-on methodology has allowed me to develop not only technical skills, but also strong problem-solving ability and adaptability in unfamiliar environments.

Experience & initiatives

My experience extends beyond personal projects. Mindset & balance

I describe myself as a disciplined, curious, and creativity-driven individual with a strong internal motivation to improve continuously. While I am actively working on enhancing my focus and time management, I compensate through consistency, structured self-learning, and a strong commitment to long-term goals.

Outside of technology, I maintain balance through interests such as football, gaming, anime, and music, which contribute to my creativity and perspective.

Direction

My short-term objective is to deepen my expertise in cybersecurity, advance Tasklyst.io, and continue building impactful technical projects. Ultimately, I am driven by a clear goal: financial independence, continuous growth, and the ability to create meaningful impact for myself and my family through technology and innovation.

tasklyst.io

Tasklyst.io is a single-page site that acts as a central hub for 50+ small web utilities—converters, formatters, calculators, and similar tools.

The hero groups everything into Developers, Designers, and Others, with search, an in-page tool viewer (including split view for two tools), and a settings panel to adjust the neon accent.
